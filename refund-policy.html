Refund Policy

Home ~ Refund Policy

At Innovate Axis, we strive to provide high-quality web solutions and services, including web development, hosting, and technical support. Our goal is to ensure customer satisfaction with every project we undertake. However, we understand that there may be circumstances where a refund request is necessary. This Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which refunds may be requested and processed.

1. Refund Eligibility

Refunds may be issued under the following conditions:

  • Web Development Services: Once the work has commenced on your website design or development, no refund will be issued unless explicitly agreed upon in advance. If there is a delay or issue with the project delivery, we will work with you to resolve the issue. Refunds for incomplete projects may be considered on a case-by-case basis, depending on the extent of the work completed.

  • Hosting Services: Refund requests for hosting services will be accepted within 30 days of the initial payment. If you are not satisfied with the hosting services provided, you may request a refund within this 30-day period. After 30 days, no refund will be provided, as hosting services are considered used.

  • Domain Registration & Transfer: All domain registration and transfer services are non-refundable once the process has begun. This includes domain renewals, as these are completed services with no refund eligibility.

2. Refund Process

If you meet the criteria for a refund, the following process will apply:

  • Submit your refund request via email to innovateaxis@gmail.com. Please include your order number, payment details, and a clear explanation of the reason for your request.
  • Our team will review your request and determine if it meets our refund criteria. Refund requests will be processed within 7-10 business days after the review.

3. Non-Refundable Services

The following services are non-refundable:

  • Any additional services or add-ons that were requested after the initial service agreement.
  • Payments for domain names, SSL certificates, and any custom-designed work.
  • Services that have already been rendered, such as completed web development projects or ongoing hosting plans after the 30-day refund window.

4. Partial Refunds

In some cases, if a refund is requested after services have been partially rendered, a partial refund may be provided. This will depend on the work already completed, and the refund amount will be determined based on the time and resources spent on your project.

5. Payment Method

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the transaction. If payment was made via credit card, the refund will be processed to the same credit card account. If payment was made via bank transfer or another method, the refund will be issued according to our records.

6. Changes to the Refund Policy

Innovate Axis reserves the right to modify, amend, or update this Refund Policy at any time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage our clients to review this policy periodically.
